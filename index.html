About Me

Hi there! My name is Steven and I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer. I've always found joy in creating, building, and figuring out how things work. So it was almost inevitable that I'd end up in the world of Web Development eventually. I'm always up for a new challenge, and I'm also easily adaptable as well as a quick learner. There are three core tenets that I place importance upon:

Engineering

Building web applications with thoughtful planning, methodical thinking, and quality testing. My goal is to produce code that is clear, concise, and abides by the best practices and conventions.

UX Principles

Considering and understanding the end-user through smart UX practices is crucial, and can be improved by user mapping, wireframing, high accessibility, and effective communication.

Visual Design

Designs and interfaces that are sharp, intuitive, meaningful, and responsive to a wide variety of devices. Content arranged with proper structure, hierarchy, and consistency.
